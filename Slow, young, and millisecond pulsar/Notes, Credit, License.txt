
# Notes:
These are three animations of 1-Hz, 11-Hz, and 650-Hz pulsars.
The accompanying sounds were modified from recordings at WSRT, Parkes and Jodrell on B0329+54, Vela and B1937+21.

Source files are Autodesk Maya and After Effects scenes, and sound file.
